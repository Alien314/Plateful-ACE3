## 1. Overview
Allows a tank commander to re-aim the main turret or to aim their turret at the what the main turret is looking at

## 2. Usage

### 2.1 Commander Override
- Place the crosshair on the enemy vehicle.
- Press <kbd>Q</kbd>

### 2.2 Commander Observe
- Press <kbd>E</kbd>
